EGG HUNT

THE GAME CAN BE STARTED WHEN 2 OR MORE PLAYERS ARE CLOSE TO EACH OTHER.

THE OBJECTIVE OF THE GAME IS TO COLLECT AS MANY EGGS AS POSSIBLE BEFORE THE TIME IS UP, THE PLAYER WITH THE MOST POINTS WINS.

TO COLLECT AN EGG YOU JUST HAVE TO MOVE YOURSELF (AND YOUR PHONE) TO THE EGG'S COORDINATES.
